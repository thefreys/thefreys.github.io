# Bing, Please give me a recipe for crab cakes using imitation crab Certainly! Here is a recipe for **Imitation Crab Meat Cakes** that you can try: Ingredients: - 1 pound imitation crab meat - 1/2 cup Ritz cracker crumbs - 1/4 cup mayonnaise - 1 egg, slightly beaten - 1/4 cup onion, chopped - 1/4 teaspoon salt - 1/4 teaspoon black pepper - 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder - 1/4 teaspoon dried parsley - 1/4 cup vegetable oil Instructions: 1. Crush the Ritz crackers in a ziplock bag and set aside. 2. Shred the imitation crab meat and place it in a mixing bowl. 3. Add the mayonnaise, slightly beaten egg, chopped onion, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and dried parsley to the mixing bowl. 4. Mix all the ingredients well. 5. Add the crushed Ritz crackers to the mixing bowl and mix again. 6. Form the mixture into 8 equal crab cakes. 7. Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. 8. Fry the crab cakes in the skillet until golden brown on both sides. 9. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
