# Bard, Please give me a recipe for crab cakes using imitation crab Here's a delicious recipe for crab cakes using imitation crab: **Ingredients:** * 12 ounces imitation crab meat, lump or claw meat preferred * 1/2 cup mayonnaise * 1/4 cup panko breadcrumbs * 1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper * 1/4 cup finely chopped green onion * 1 egg, beaten * 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard * 1 teaspoon lemon juice * 1/2 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning * 1/4 teaspoon salt * 1/4 teaspoon black pepper * Canola oil for frying **Instructions:** 1. In a large bowl, combine the imitation crab meat, mayonnaise, panko breadcrumbs, red bell pepper, green onion, egg, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, Old Bay seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Mix gently until just combined. [Image of Imitation crab meat] [Image of Panko breadcrumbs] [Image of Red bell pepper] [Image of Green onion] 2. Form the mixture into 6 equal patties. 3. Heat about 1/4 inch of canola oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 4. Add the crab cakes to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through. 5. Serve immediately with your favorite dipping sauce, such as tartar sauce, remoulade, or aioli. **Tips:** * For a crispier crust, you can coat the crab cakes in additional breadcrumbs before frying. * If you don't have panko breadcrumbs, you can use regular breadcrumbs. * You can also bake the crab cakes instead of frying them.
