Here's a simple yet delicious recipe for you to try: ### Beef Stew Recipe #### Ingredients: - 2 pounds beef chuck, cut into 1.5-inch cubes - 1/4 cup all-purpose flour - 2 tablespoons olive oil - 1 large onion, chopped - 3-4 cloves garlic, minced - 1/2 cup red wine (optional) - 4 cups beef broth - 2 tablespoons tomato paste - 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce - 1 teaspoon dried thyme - 1 teaspoon dried rosemary - 2 bay leaves - 3 large carrots, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces - 2 large potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes - Salt and pepper to taste - Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish) #### Instructions: 1. **Prepare the Beef:** - Pat the beef dry with paper towels. Season with salt and pepper, then toss with flour until coated. 2. **Brown the Beef:** - Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the beef in batches, being careful not to overcrowd the pot. Brown on all sides, then transfer to a plate. 3. **Saut√© the Vegetables:** - In the same pot, add the onion and cook until translucent. Add the garlic and cook for an additional minute. 4. **Deglaze and Simmer:** - Pour in the red wine (if using) and scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. Add the beef broth, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, rosemary, and bay leaves. Bring to a simmer. 5. **Cook the Beef:** - Return the beef to the pot. Cover and simmer for about 1.5 hours, or until the beef is starting to become tender. 6. **Add Vegetables:** - Add the carrots and potatoes to the pot. Simmer for another 30 minutes or until the vegetables and beef are tender. 7. **Final Seasoning and Serve:** - Remove the bay leaves. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley. Enjoy your homemade beef stew with some crusty bread or over a bed of rice or noodles for a complete and satisfying meal!
