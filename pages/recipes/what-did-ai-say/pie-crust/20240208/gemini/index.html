Here are some additional tips for making pie crust: * Use all-purpose flour for the best results. * Make sure your butter is very cold before cutting it into the flour. You can even freeze it for 30 minutes before using. * Add the water a tablespoon at a time, stopping once the dough just comes together. Don't overmix! * Chill the dough for at least 30 minutes before rolling it out. This will help it hold its shape and prevent shrinking in the oven. * When rolling out the dough, use a light touch and avoid overworking it. * If the dough tears, simply patch it up with your fingers.
